This paper was written in the fall of 2016 as part of the semester project for the 7\textsuperscript{th} semester at Aalborg University.
It was written under the supervision of Anders Bruun.
We thank ITS and specifically Nils Østbjerg and Aage Sørensen, for their help with servers used in development.

This paper should be viewed in colour, as most of the graphs and figures uses colour to differentiate objects.
Moreover the paper was submitted electronically alongside a zip--file, containing all code, tests, and API documentation developed during this project.
